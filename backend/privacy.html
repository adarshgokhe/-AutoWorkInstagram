Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Anime Nova Manager is an Instagram Business account management tool for the Instagram account @funzone_creator. The app helps create and publish original or royalty-free anime-style content, generate safe captions, and respond to Instagram comments or direct messages that users send to the account.

Information We Access

When connected through official Meta and Instagram APIs, the app may access basic Instagram business account information, media publishing status, comments, direct message conversations, sender identifiers, message text, timestamps, and related webhook event data needed to generate and send a reply.

How We Use Information

Information is used only to operate the Instagram account, generate safe anime-brand replies, block spam, abusive, scam, or adult content, keep activity logs, and improve future safe content planning. The app does not sell personal data, does not buy or fake engagement, and does not send mass spam messages.

Publishing and Content Safety

The app is designed to publish original, user-owned, licensed, or royalty-free media only. It does not intentionally scrape copyrighted anime content, remove other creators' watermarks, or post 18+ / NSFW content.

Data Storage and Security

Access tokens and app secrets are stored in the server environment and are not shown publicly. Local logs may store message text, reply text, status, and webhook metadata for troubleshooting and account operation. Access to the dashboard is password protected.

Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Data may be processed through official Meta APIs and trusted AI/API providers only when needed to generate a safe reply or operate the account. We do not share data for advertising resale or unrelated third-party marketing.

Data Retention

Operational logs are kept only as long as needed for account management, troubleshooting, and safety review. Data can be deleted when it is no longer needed or when a valid deletion request is received.

User Choices and Deletion

Users can request deletion of stored message or comment data related to them. See the Data Deletion Instructions.

Contact

For privacy or deletion requests, contact the owner of @funzone_creator through Instagram or through the contact email listed in the Meta app settings.
