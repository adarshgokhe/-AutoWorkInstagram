Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Anime Nova Manager is used to manage the Instagram Business account @funzone_creator. If you have sent a direct message or comment to this Instagram account and want related stored data deleted, follow the instructions below.

How to Request Deletion

Send a message to @funzone_creator on Instagram with this text:

Delete my data

You may also contact the app owner through the contact email listed in the Meta app settings. Include your Instagram username and state that you want Anime Nova Manager data deleted.

What Will Be Deleted

We will delete locally stored message text, comment text, generated reply text, sender identifiers, webhook metadata, and troubleshooting logs related to your Instagram interaction when it can be reasonably identified.

What May Be Retained

Some data may remain in Meta/Instagram systems according to Meta's own data retention rules. We may keep minimal records if required for security, abuse prevention, or legal compliance.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are normally processed within 30 days after we receive enough information to identify the data.

Contact

Contact @funzone_creator on Instagram or use the contact email listed in the Meta app settings.
